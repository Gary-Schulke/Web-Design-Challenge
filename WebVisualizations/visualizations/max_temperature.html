Maximum Temperature

November 17, 2019


The data samples were taken about a month before the winter solstice and the temperatures shown reflect this. The southern hemisphere temperatures near 80 degrees between -20 and -30 degrees latitude is more concentrated that the northern hemiphere temperatures between +20 and +30 degrees latitude. It is also interesting to look at the outliers of the coldest and hottest temperatures. The hottest location is below the equator in Rio Tercero, Argentina with a temperature of 93.88 degrees F. The coldest location was in Nadym, Russia with a temperature of -24.93 degrees F. Observe the general readings of temperature at -30 degrees and +30 degrees. Winter is about to arrive in the north.
