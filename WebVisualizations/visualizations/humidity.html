Humidity

November 17, 2019

Humidity readings do not have the same north/south bias seen in the temperature data. Humidity readings are well dispersed between hemipheres and latitudes. It is important to remember that the readings are relative humidity meaning that it is a measurement of how saturated the atmosphere is with respect to air temperature. In the north, it's not the rainy season in most parts of the world and temperatures are generally lower. While the relative humidity might be high, the volume of water captured by the atmosphere is less than in warmer areas. If you like low humidity, Cervo, Spain has the lowest reading at 1% relative humidity.
